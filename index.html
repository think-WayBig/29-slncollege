Comprehensive Academic Excellence

Our institution, Shree Lakshmi Narayan Ayurvedic College & Hospital, strictly adheres to the academic programs prescribed by the National Commission for Indian System of Medicine (NCISM) employing advanced teaching methodologies like Miller's Pyramid and Bloom's Taxonomy to ensure a well-rounded education in Ayurvedic principles and practices.

Extensive Clinical Experience

Students gain hands-on experience in our 60-bedded hospital, working directly with patients under the supervision of experienced faculty, coupled with a well-equipped operation theatre, labor room, X-ray department, and clinical laboratory, ensures they are well-prepared for real-world clinical scenarios.

Exceptional Career Opportunities

Our institute's strong reputation, established since 1972 and managed by Shree Durgiana Education Foundation, provides graduates with numerous career opportunities in Ayurveda with a history of producing renowned Ayurvedic professionals and securing many P.G. seats both in Punjab and throughout India.
