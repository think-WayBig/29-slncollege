General Hospital

Shree Lakshmi Narayan Ayurvedic College & Hospital, Amritsar is situated in the heart of the city at Sant Tulsidass Marg near Durgiana Mandir O/s Lohgarh gate, Amritsar. It is a 60 bedded hospital. Apart from it there are 2 beds in private room and 2 beds in Emergency section. The College is running regular OPDs of all specialties. Patients with various chronic diseases are being cured here successfully. Facilities of OT and Labour room are also available here.

Various OPD’s of different specialties give their services to the patients under the expert hands of qualified and specialist doctors. We are having 7 OPD’s:

  • Kayachikitsa
  • Panchakarma
  • Shalya Tantra
  • Shalakya Tantra
  • Prasuti Tantra Evum Stree Roga
  • Kaumarabhritya/Bal Roga
  • Swastha Vritta/Yoga & Physiotherapy

Departments

1. Department of Kaya Chikitsa

Consulting Doctors (Faculty)
  1. Dr. Ritesh Chawla M.D. (Ay) Prof.
  2. Dr. Priyanka Bhatt M.D. (Ay) Lecturer
  3. Dr. Pallavi Sharma M.D. (Ay) Lecturer

This is the prime branch of Ayurveda which is akin to General Medicine in Ayurveda. This department not only deals with the curative measures i.e. with medicines but also the preventive aspects too. It deals with the diseases by means of Shodhana (Purification), Shamana (Pacification) and Satwawajaya (Counselling).

A regular functioning OPD and IPD of this department deals not only with acute but also the chronic diseases and also make it feasible for the students to learn those skills properly with ample practical exposure.

  1. Dr. Rajeev Sood M.D. (Ay) Prof and Head
  2. Dr. Varun Bhagat M.D. (Ay) Lecturer

Department of Panchakarma is the core heart of Hospital. Our department is constructively engaged in the revival of an affordable, reliable and proven Ayurvedic health care system.

Motto: – To create an environment where individuals and groups of people are healed, rejuvenated and re-energized to experience a healthy way of life. All Panchakarma procedures are carried out by trained therapists with the complete availability for carrying out procedures. In addition to this Keraliya Panchakarma is also carried out here. We perform more than 10,000 therapies every year.

3. Department of Shalya Tantra (General Surgery)

Consulting Doctors:
  1. Dr. Paramjit Singh M.S. (Ay) Reader
  2. Dr. Ankit Saini M.S. (Ay) Lecturer

Shalya Tantra is one of the main branches of Ayurveda which deals with the diagnosis and management of diseases that require conservative, para-surgical or surgical intervention.

Facilities Available:
  • Outdoor Patient facility for all surgical disorders like Piles, Fistula, Appendix, Hernia etc.
  • Kshara Sutra treatment for Piles, Fistula-in-Ano, Fissure & Pilonidal sinus with less reoccurrence rate.
  • Agnikarma (Thermal Cauterization) & Kshara Karma (Chemical Cauterization) for surgical disorders.
  • Blood Letting by leeches (Jalouka) & Sira Vedha ( Vene Puncture ) is also carried out in Shalya Department.
  • All general surgical Procedures are also carried out here.

4. Department of Shalakya Tantra

Consulting Doctors (Faculty):
  1. Dr. Tipu M.S (Ay) Lecturer
  2. Dr. Amandeep Gupta M.S (Ay) Lecturer
Facilities Available
  • Diagnosis & Treatment of disorders related with shalakya (Eye & Ent) above the neck i.e. Udharvanga Chikitsa.
  • Occular Physiotherapy & Yoga specially for eye sight.
  • Kriya kalpa ( Akshi Tarpana, Karna Purna, Anjana , Gandusha, Kavala, Nasya etc)
  • Accupressure & Accupuncture.
  • All essential instruments are available in the department for diagnosis of various kinds of problems.

This department deals with the treatment of diseases related to the parts of the body situated above the neck. These are mainly the diseases of Ear, Nose, Throat, Oral cavity and eyes. Shalakya Tantra covers most of the specialties which are coming under the purview of modern E.N.T.

5. Department of Prasuti Tantra Evum Stree Roga

Consulting Doctors (Faculty):
  1. Dr: Jasbir Kaur M.D (Ay) Professor
  2. Dr. Mandeep Kaur M.S.(Ay) Lecturer
Facilities Available
  • Labour Room for conducting deliveries
  • Functional O-T for Cesarean Section
  • Uttarbasti for various gynecological disorders

Special work and trials are being done on the patients of PCOD, Infertility and TORCH positive patients by the Ayurvedic Medicine and procedures like Uttarbasti and vaginal douching.

This department deals with the treatment of antenatal care of pregnant women, post-natal care, diseases related to female reproductive system & management of labour. The department of Prasuti Tantra evam Stree Roga has facilities for the management of all Gynecological diseases including Antenatal, Natal, and Post-natal care with the advanced techniques and Ayurveda modalities of treatment.

6. Department of Kaumarabhritya / Bal Roga

Consulting Doctors (Faculty):
  1. Dr.Sudha Bhalla M.D. (Ay) Reader
  2. Dr.Ipsha M.D. (Ay) Lecturer

Kaumarbhritya is one of the most important branches amongst ayurveda as it deals with the prevention and cure of childhood diseases. This department deals with the treatment of diseases like: Balaroga (Pediatrics), Prasooti Tantra (Obstetrics), Stree Roga (Gynecology) etc.

This department also takes care of the health of children from neonates to adolescents. The department also takes care of the health of mothers from antenatal to postnatal care.

Department is running regular OPD and providing the following facilities:
  • Management of growth and development disorders.
  • Deals in allergic disorders like skin, respiratory disorders etc.
  • Diagnosis and management as well as counselling of child and parents for various behavioral and psychological disorders like ADHD, showing trantrums, Bed wetting etc.
  • Vaccination is also going under department every Monday as per National Immunization Schedule.
  • Pediatric Panchakarma for Neuro-muscular disorders like Paralysis, Cerebral palsy is carried out.
  • Suvarna Prashna sanskara is under trial.
  • Treatment of General diseases through Ayurvedic Medicine.

It also deals with the treatment of various diseases through Yoga and Naturopathy. The regular OPD of Swasthavritta & Yoga Department is running successfully in our hospital.
